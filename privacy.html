Privacy

How SkyLayer collects, uses and protects information when you request a quote or use our services.

Privacy policy (summary)

SkyLayer provides websites, hosting and maintenance services. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your options.

Data we collect

  • Contact requests: name, email, phone (optional), and the details you send us.
  • Project information: content, images, access credentials you share for deployment or integrations.
  • Technical logs: server and security logs (IP, timestamps, user-agent) for reliability and abuse prevention.
  • Analytics (optional): aggregated usage metrics if you enable analytics.

How we use data

  • To respond to quotes and deliver the service.
  • To maintain and secure websites and hosting.
  • To improve performance and reliability.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to operate the service (hosting, email delivery, analytics), and only to the extent required. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

We keep contact and project data for as long as needed to provide support and meet legal/operational obligations. You can request deletion of non-essential data.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies for site functionality. Optional analytics cookies are used only if analytics is enabled.

Your choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Ask what data we have about you.
